Beef Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Brown beef cubes on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add tomato paste, thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir

  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, until beef is tender.
  7. Remove bay leaf before serving.
